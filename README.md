# Diondria-Morris-AI-Healthcare
Healthcare is one of the most data intensive industries in the world and artificial intelligence is fundamentally changing what's possible within it. Diagnostic models are detecting disease earlier with greater consistency. Clinical decision support systems are giving practitioners tools that sharpen the quality of care at the point of decision. Natural language processing is turning years of unstructured patient data into something actionable. The possibilities stretch further every year, and the field is still in its early stages. I'm a senior AI student finishing a Bachelor of Applied Technology in Artificial Intelligence with a genuine interest in how the subfields of machine learning are being applied across industries, healthcare being one of the most consequential. This portfolio represents the work I completed in AI in Healthcare. A course that pushed me to engage with the field seriously; from its technical foundations to its tangible implications.
